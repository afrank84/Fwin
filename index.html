    About Me

    I build really cool things for the web. It is the best medium in the world to reach mass individuals. I love advertising and marketing, and now use that same medium (the internet) as my go to choice for helping my clients reach their best potential.

    If you want a website that you can be proud of and not ashamed to hand to your clients, then you came to the right place. If you want to build it yourself, go for it. I love the DIY type, just don't expect perfection over night. I have been coding and building things for the web since I was 15 years old. I spend on average easily over a 100 man hours studying web languages, best practices, and all things that will help your business grow.
